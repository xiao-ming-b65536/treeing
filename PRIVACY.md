# Privacy Policy

Treeing is a fully local, offline desktop application. It does not connect to the internet, does not upload any data, and does not collect personal information.

## What is stored on your computer

The application stores a small set of interface preferences in a single file:

- font size,
- the last output directory used for generation,
- the default file-import encoding,
- the list of encodings shown in the import drop-down.

This file sits at `~/.treeing/settings.json` (a `.treeing` folder inside your home directory). It exists only to restore your preferences on the next launch, and its contents never leave your machine.

## What is not collected

Treeing uses no cookies, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party services. No information about you or your use of the application is transmitted to the author or to anyone else.

## Your choices

You may delete `~/.treeing/settings.json` at any time; the application will then fall back to its default settings and create a fresh file when needed. You may also inspect or edit the file with any text editor while the application is closed.

## Scope

This policy covers the Treeing application itself, whether obtained as source or as a prebuilt executable from the project's official release page. It does not cover any third-party platform from which you may have downloaded the application; the policy of that platform applies separately.

## Contact

Questions may be raised through the issue tracker on the project's official homepage.
